Best Burgers and Ice Cream in Town!

Welcome to Dairy Bar! We’re a locally owned burger and ice cream restaurant conveniently located in the heart of Red River, New Mexico. We offer a variety of:

  • burgers
  • sandwiches
  • wraps
  • soups
  • salads
  • ice cream

You can order carry-out or grab a table in our friendly and comfortable dining room. All of our food is prepared fresh to order with only the finest ingredients, and we work very hard to give you the best possible food experience.

Our specialties are our hand-crafted burgers and sandwiches, and of course our legendary milkshakes, but whatever you order, it’s sure to be delicious! Click below to order online, or come by and see us soon!
